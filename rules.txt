Initialize tokens
----------------- 
Attendee    - 0 
Vendor      - 10000 

Transactions
------------
- Give 
1. Vendor gives Attendee token 
2. VMware gives Vendor 10000 tokens at start

- Redeem
Attendee gets a prize based on how many tokens they have

- Get Balance
1. Attendee can check how many tokens they have
2. Vender can see how many people have stopped by their booth
